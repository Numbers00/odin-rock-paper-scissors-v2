Rock Paper Scissors!

A battle royale! 64 Contestants pick their avatar and fight to the death... with rock paper scissors! Contestants will face a random opponent each time, the first to reach a score of 5 survives and moves on to face the next! This goes on until one is left standing and is hailed champion! The common rules apply, rock beats scissors and so on. Starting with a timer of 10 seconds, the timer decrementing by 1 each round. Contestants reserve the right to surrender each round at the expense of their prize. Without further ado, let the games begin!
